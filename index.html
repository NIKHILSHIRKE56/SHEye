🔍 What is SHEye?

SHEye is a smart AI system that analyzes CCTV feeds in real-time to detect harassment, stalking, physical violence, and accidents. Upon detection, it sends instant alerts via WhatsApp, SMS, or email — ensuring rapid intervention without any human delay.

⚙️ Technologies Used

YOLOv8 · OpenCV · Python · FastAPI · Streamlit · Twilio API · WhatsApp Automation

🚀 Why SHEye?

Because every woman deserves to walk safely through public spaces. SHEye aims to bring proactive, AI-driven protection into every metro, bus stop, and station.
